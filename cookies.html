Cookie Policy

How LTD Nuvio Gear uses cookies and tracking technologies on nuviogear.com.

This Cookie Policy explains how LTD Nuvio Gear ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit nuviogear.com. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy, which provides broader information about how we handle your personal data.

Last updated: June 25, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, work more efficiently, and to provide information to the website owners.

Cookies can be "session" cookies, which are deleted when you close your browser, or "persistent" cookies, which remain on your device for a set period of time or until you manually delete them. They can be set by the website you are visiting ("first-party cookies") or by third-party services used by that website ("third-party cookies").

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Site functionality: To enable core features such as session management, preference storage (e.g. dismissed banners), and ensuring the site displays correctly on your device.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how visitors use our site — such as which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, and where they come from. This helps us improve the site. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics cookies.
  • Security: Some cookies help us detect and prevent fraudulent activity and protect the integrity of the website.

3. Types of cookies we use

Type Purpose Can be disabled?
Essential Cookies Required for the website to function. Include session management, security tokens, and basic preferences. No — disabling these will prevent the site from working correctly.
Analytics Cookies Google Analytics collects anonymous data on site usage — pages visited, time on site, traffic sources. Used to improve content and performance. Yes — opt out via browser settings or the Google Analytics opt-out tool.
Preference Cookies Remember your choices on the site, such as whether you have dismissed the announcement banner. Yes — can be cleared by deleting cookies in your browser.

4. Third party cookies

We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics sets cookies to collect information about how you use our website. The information generated is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers that may be located outside your country.

Google uses this information on our behalf to evaluate your use of the website and compile reports on website activity. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf.

You can learn more about how Google uses data by visiting policies.google.com/privacy.

5. How to control cookies

You have the right to accept or reject cookies. You can control and manage cookies in the following ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to view, manage, delete, and block cookies. Visit your browser's help section for instructions. Note that blocking all cookies may affect site functionality.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: To prevent Google Analytics from collecting data about your visit, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Clearing cookies: You can delete existing cookies from your browser at any time. This will remove any preference data stored on your device.

Please be aware that restricting cookies may impact the functionality of our website and your experience on it.

6. Do not track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want your online activity tracked. At this time, there is no universally accepted standard for responding to DNT signals.

We do not currently alter our data collection or use practices in response to DNT signals from your browser. If a standard is established in the future, we will review our practices accordingly.

7. Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are using cookies. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
