Our Story

Gear Born on
the Road

Nuvio Gear was founded in 2020 by a team of three engineers and Delivery service managers in Tallinn, Estonia. We spent two years seeing couriers riding the same routes, carrying the same gear, and suffering the same failures: thermal bags that lost temperature, backpacks that shredded after three months, safety vests that weren't visible enough.

So we built something better. Starting with one thermal bag prototype tested across seven cities, we iterated relentlessly until couriers told us it was the best piece of equipment they'd ever used. Today, Nuvio Gear equips over 100,000 couriers and 46+ partner companies across 18 EU countries.

We don't design for catalogues. We design for 8-hour shifts in the rain, for emergency stops on cobblestones, for sub-zero winters and 35°C summers. Every stitch, every buckle, every insulation layer is tested by working couriers in real conditions.

Milestones

Our Journey

2020

Founded in Tallinn

Three courier-Managers-turned-engineers meet at a hackathon and decide the courier equipment market needs to be disrupted. First prototype built in a garage.

2021

First Product Launch

NG-Thermo Pro 40L launches after 14 months of field testing. Sells out within 3 weeks. 500 units delivered to couriers in Tallinn, Helsinki, and Riga.

2022

Series A & EU Expansion

Nuvio Gear expands to 12 EU countries. First fleet partnership with large marketplace signed for more than 10K branded units.

2023

100,000 Couriers Equipped

Major milestone reached. Full catalogue of 42 products across 12 categories launched. B2B fleet program scaled to 46+ partner companies across Europe.

2024

Guaranteed Quality

Custom manufacturing facility opens in Tallinn Industrial Park.

2025

18 Countries & Growing

Nuvio Gear now ships to 18 EU countries with 1-3 months delivery. New product lines in development for cargo bike and moped couriers.
